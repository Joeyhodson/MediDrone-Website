Divide & Conquer

 The Divide & Conquer document marked the team's initial research and planning stage, where they explored various project possibilities. After a lot of discussions, the team decided to settle on developing a medical delivery drone. Although the drone underwent several derivations throughout the course of the project, the core themes and ideas explored during the initial research persisted until the final prototype.
